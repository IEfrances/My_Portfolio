QR Hunter

Overview

Role: Product developer

Project type: Android Game application

Programming Language: Java

Project length: 4 months

Team size: 6 members

Year: 2023

QR Hunter is an engaging Android mobile game application designed to provide players with a unique and interactive gaming experience. The game centers around the concept of scanning QR codes at various locations, allowing players to accumulate points and compete for rankings against other participants.
